Government Schemes for Textile Industry Finance Assistance

Central Government Schemes

  1. Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
    • Subsidized loans up to ₹25 lakh for micro-enterprises in the textile sector.
    • Official Website: PMEGP Guidelines
  2. Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)
    • Collateral-free loans for MSMEs up to ₹2 crore.
    • Resources: CGTMSE Website

State Government Schemes (Delhi)

  1. Delhi MSME Facilitation Council
    • Provides financial assistance and guidance for textile industry entrepreneurs.
